Wellbeing

The main goal of my wellbeing plan is to help reduce stress and difficulty over bootcamp.

Some of my key needs at the moment would be rest and energy. I can facilitate this by making sure I get to bed within a reasonable time, and during the day to take breaks when possible, especially away from the screen, if it's nice I could go outside and get some fresh air.

When I start getting stressed, I find it hard to focus on anything else, and my window of tolerance shrinks a lot, I'm more likely to get annoyed or irritated at others. What I need, or what might help, would be some time to calm down and refocus, a word of encouragement, understanding, patience, and empathy.

Something I've been doing is taking the time to focus on something non course-related, just relaxing with a book, spending time with family, or going on a walk. This helps clear my brain and improves my mood. I've also been working on increasing my self-compassion
